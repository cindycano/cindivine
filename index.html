FAQ

I'm unsure what reading to ask for.

That's perfectly fine! Many people are interested in the insight tarot has to offer, but are unsure on how to begin. In these cases, I'd recommend just asking for a general reading, or even a general reading in a certain area of life!

How long does a reading take?

A typical reading takes anywhere from 1 day to 3 weeks. It all depends on how many people are on the waitlist and how many readings you require.

How much do you charge?

Good news: As of right now, readings are free! Although not necessary, many clients desire to support my services and thus donations are now accepted! At the moment I am more focused on helping clients and improving my readings, but still keep in mind the energy and time that it takes to do tarot!

How does tarot work?

Tarot is a tool used to communicate with the divine. Personally, for a tarot reading I'll call on the client's Spirit Guides, Angels, and/or Spirit itself to help provide the answers you seek. Nothing weird. Nothing scary. Just a nice safe conversation with the divine. :)

Is tarot evil?

Not at all! Tarot cards are simply paper with pretty pictures on them. They are tools to connect with the divine, making it easier to communicate. Receiving messages through tarot is no different than hearing an answer from a prayer or receiving signs in your daily life.
